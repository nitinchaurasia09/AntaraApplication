The Antara Way

The Antara Way is our guiding principle to conceive and craft a beautifully designed community for the progressive few who believe age is jsut a number. Life as we get older is a time for renewal and reflections, a time to reconnect with ourselves and forge new bonds within a safe, inspiring and well managed environment. It is a time that is filled with hope and energy couples with the aspiration of less worry and a better quality of life. The vision of creating the first such community in India gave direction, shape and identity to what we call the Antara Way. The Antara Way creates a comprehensive ecosystem with four principle elements

A genuine spirit of care

The Antara Way is first and foremost about caring. Since our goal is to make every Antara resident feel well looked after and that their every need is resourcefully dealt with, our approach to your Antara life goes beyond just physical health to a perfectly balanced care of mind, body and sprit. Our layout, design, group activities and individual pursuits are tailored to enhance and promote a holistic welness in you. Our trained team of nearly 200 is always on call to provide attentive but unobtrusive service.

An eye for detail

Your Antara apartment is individually crafted, built with the finest materials and finished with meticulous attention to detail to make your Antara apartment beautifull, perfect for you and timeless. Each block is conceived to have a unique look and feel and an optimized view. Entrance lobbies are spacious and welcoming. The use of optimum materials and ideas make for a living area that's perfect for the individual resident as well as for interacting with your immediate neighbors.
